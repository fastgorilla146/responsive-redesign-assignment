Website of Choice: https://berkshirehathaway.com/

Category Description
Usability
  • There is no cohesiveness to the hyperlinks making it hard to find certain articles
  • There is no search button for the content of the articles
  • Text is very small and the description of the links is even smaller making it very difficult for those with poor eyesight
Learnability
  • Learnability is surprisingly good as the links are obvious that they are links and there are no other buttons to click
  • There are no headings which might make it difficult to figure out what users are looking at in the first place
Memorability
  • It is memorable in a bad way
  • No images or labels to remember the site by only text
  • No bright colors only navy blue and white background

WebAIM WAVE Analysis:

There were a few problems pointed out by the WebAIM WAVE. First off, there are no headings making it so there is no cohesive structure to the site. All the content is also in an unordered list with many cases of very small text. This is a problem since there is barely any content on the page so it being very small makes the page look awkwardly spaced. Overall, however, there was not many errors with the page, but that can be contributed to the very small amount of content on the page.
